Third generation refrigerator

第三代冰箱 变温卖点第三空间包装推广

The temperature change area of Haier refrigerator can achieve a temperature change of -18℃ to 7℃, giving you a variety of storage options to meet the needs of all family members.
海尔冰箱变温区域，可以实现-18℃到7℃变温，给你多种存储选择，满足所有家人的需要。

The concept of the third space is one more choice, just like between a home and a company, there is a space in a car for a person, which gives you multiple possible spaces in your life.

第三空间的概念，是多一种选择，就像家和公司之间，有一个人专属车内空间，是给你生活中多种可能的空间。

We find OK and 3 gestures, connect gestures with concepts, and use different characters, different ingredients, and different colors to express a colorful life.

我们找到创意元，OK和数字3的手势，将手势与概念联系在一起，用不同角色、不同食材、不同颜色，表现多彩生活。
