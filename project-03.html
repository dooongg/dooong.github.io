TOSHIBA Refrigerator


Live in original nature

Fresh ingredients come from the place of origin, the apples that have just been picked from the trees, the live fish caught in the river, these are the freshest states. Inspired by The Durrells, returning to nature, in the summer afternoon, the family moved the table to the sea, sitting barefoot in the Mediterranean sea for an afternoon tea, watching the sea and the blue sky. This is the state of the best life in a busy life.

Moist-Temp Control

Innovative technology in the refrigerator, the refrigerator can feel and adjust humidity and temperature. This provides a better environment for fresh ingredients and maintains the freshest state. The picture is based on this product technology.
In order to achieve the effect, from the draft to the three-dimensional graphics, and then through the Photoshop image layering.
