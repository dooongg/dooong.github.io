Independent Dry&wet fresh space


The dry and wet storage technology can keep the humidity in the wet area at about 90% and the humidity in the dry area at about 45%. Fruits and vegetables are moisturizing and non-condensing, and dry goods are not subject to moisture and deterioration.

干湿分储技术，能够使湿区湿度保持在90%左右，干区湿度保持在45%左右。水果蔬菜保湿不凝露，干货不受潮不变质。

The independent Dry&wet fresh space is like a building composed of bread, dried fruits, and cheese above, and a reflection of the building composed of fruits and vegetables below. Although both are in the same field of view, they are two separate spaces.

独立的干湿分储空间就好像上面面包、干果、奶酪组成建筑，与下面水果、蔬菜组成的水面建筑倒影。两者虽然都在一个视野里，但是独立的两个空间，也满足你全世界食材不同需求

Unlimited installation location

Another creative direction is to compare the two natural environments, the desert and the island in two separate worlds.

另一个创意方向，是通过两个自然环境对比，沙漠与海岛两个分离的世界。
